Controls:  
- Flying Movement: WASD + QE to rotate, space to move forward
- Grounded Movement: WASD to move, Space to jump, Space in midair to fly
- Firing: Right click to aim, left click to fire
- Camera Switch: F
- Transparency Toggle: T
- Invert Y Axis: Y
- Open menu: P
- Camera distance: -/= or mousewheel. Note that NumPad keys don't work.
